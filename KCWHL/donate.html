Your donation will help us grow women’s hockey in Kansas City.

Our goal is to provide opportunities for more women to enjoy hockey in a competitive yet friendly atmosphere.

The KCWHL is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization.

Your tax-deductible donation will help us make hockey more accessible by subsidizing the costs of:

  • Ice time

  • KCWHL jerseys

  • Loaner equipment

  • Travel to tournaments and regional games

  • Need-based fee waivers

Donations are processed through PayPal.
