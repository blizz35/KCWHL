KCWHL 2024 Elections

  • Saturday, July 13, 2024
  • 6:00 PM 6:00 PM
  • Google Calendar ICS

KCWHL elections will be after practice on JULY 13 at Line Creek!

We are excited to announce the upcoming elections for the positions of President and Treasurer. This is your chance to shape the future of the league and ensure its continued success and growth. Whether you have a passion for leadership or budgeting, or a desire to make a positive impact, we encourage you to run for office or support your fellow members by voting.

Want to run? E-mail KCWHL with a photograph and a paragraph about why KCWHLers should vote for you! Deadline to announce candidacy is June 29th. All interested officers will be placed on a ballot and voting will be July 13 after . Eligibility is open to all KCWHLers. This is a volunteer position and the commitment is for 2 years. The board meets every quarter for 1-2 hours and volunteer time each week/month depends on the position. Officer descriptions are on our website: https://kcwhl.com/officers

Officer position elections are staggered with Class A in 2024 and Class B elections in 2025. Class A positions are President and Treasurer. Class B positions are Vice President, Registrar, Communications. Elected officers will serve for a two year term.

If you have any questions feel free to reach out to one of the board members.

If you can't make it in person you can vote using an absentee ballot. Request one by emailing kcwomenshockey@gmail.com

-KCWHL Board Members

3rd Annual Summit Ice Pond Hockey Classic

  • Sunday, February 18, 2024
  • 8:00 AM 11:45 AM
  • Summit Ice (map)
  • Google Calendar ICS

Get ready for the 3rd Annual Pond Hockey Classic!

Register and pay by January 21 on benchapp.

We are changing some things up this year. This year we will have two divisions: the big dogs (upper level) and the lil’ doggies (lower level). Players can pick their teams or sign up as free agents. Teams will consist of three players all in the same division.

Big Dogs: Can skate backwards/crossover/stop. Solid grasp of the fundamentals- stick handling, shooting, passing, high D and C league

Lil’ Doggies: newer players, learning how to skate backward/crossovers/stop, Z league, low D

*If you have a question about which division you are in send Kate or Nicole a message and we can help.* Be honest in your assessment of your division level. KCWHL has the right to move a person up or down based on our assessment.

We will be collecting donations for an Always & Furever project!

Pond hockey t-shirts will be available to purchase with all proceeds going to Always & Furever. More details to come.

Once you have your team, email kcwomenshockey@gmail.com to secure your spot.

$40 for 3 hours of play ($20 for season pass holders).

Start time and price may change depending on how many players sign up.


Check out our gallery to view pics from past years amazingly fun tournaments.
